“Everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related than distant things.” - Tobler, 1970 (gisgeography)

Maps show us our world and enable our understanding of place.

Inspired by nature, my love of geography is driven by my quest to seek knowledge of our ecological world.

My expertise in applied geography is focused on natural resource conservation.

I am continually challenged and humbled by applying spatial analyses and cartography to empower people regarding their place in our shared environment.
